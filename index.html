Who are we?

Founded in the late 90's by Vector von an italian national, the pizza place then known as La Pizza, was located in the city of Nairobi taking up the entire place complex building owned by the kenyan government. Over the years, they ammased quite a tangible customer base attracting tourists from all walks of the world to taste their traditional pizza recipes, needless to say gaining quite some numbers in terms of money, buying out the whole place complex from the government and rebranding themselves to Pizza Place.

5 consecutive years of winning the african top consumer brands, earned us the golden pizza award.
